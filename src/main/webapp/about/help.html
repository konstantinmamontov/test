This demo search covers about 1.400.000 articles from russian Wikipedia on the date 1 September 2017

Step 1: DEFINE A TOPIC
Define topic, then select and keep facts if they look like what you mean

Step 2: MORE FACTS
This click will bring more facts on this topic
If you see fact similar to what you are looking for, keep this fact
Selected facts will help the system to understand what you mean
After the selection some facts next click on "Step 2: MORE FACTS" will bring more relevant facts

Step 3: EXPORT FACTS
Get the facts by email (if you are logged in)
